## 会议概览 / Overview
[cite_start]本次会议有来自清华大学、武汉大学、香港科技大学等20余所科研单位的30余名专家学者参加。会议围绕随机动力系统和机器学习等新兴领域的最新研究成果展开研讨 [cite: 23]。
Over 30 experts and scholars from more than 20 research institutions, including Tsinghua University and HKUST, attended the meeting to discuss the latest research results in emerging fields such as stochastic dynamical systems and machine learning.
